The Game

Are you ready to become a legend?

It is a fun 3D action-adventure shooter, based on Blockchain technology, combining NFT elements to create a Play to Earn ecosystem. Fight, do quests, get rewards and collect Skins. You can also become a Landlord, build your empire, earn income and contribute to a circular economy based on supply and demand, just like the real world! Discover a world full of adventure in the ToysLand metaverse. Move, aim and shoot as nimbly as you can to become a legend.

Game Modes

Choose your favorite mode and have fun!

Survival (PVE)

Face waves of enemies and stay on your feet as long as possible: the further you get, the more rewards you will achieve.

Multiplayer (PVP)

Choose your favorite game mode, bet and fight with your friends or other players. The winning team wins the grand prize.

Battle Royale

Go on an adventure in a free-for-all or in team matches, where the last one standing will be the big winner.

Our Nfts

Trade, collect, and above all, play!

Characters

The base character is a NFT element, which you will need to play. These characters have random stats, which you can improve through skill cards and skins that will also change your visual appearance.

Skins

These are NFT elements that change your character’s appearance, improve his stats and additionally give you a special ability that you can unlock depending on the game mode. This ability can be attack, defense, healing or stun, and visually it will depend on each skin. The skins are accessories for the base character, that is to say, you need a character to dress up. We will be adding new skins, some of them very limited!

Weapons

These are combat NFT elements that will help you complete missions, defeat your enemies and continue on your path to glory. Each weapon has different stats that will allow you to improve your damage, accuracy and speed.

Skill Cards

These NFT cards will allow you to increase your character’s abilities. Their levels range from 5 to 30; Its use will be limited to one ability of the character (who has 15 in total). So if you want to increase the stats on all of them, you’ll need to purchase more cards.

Lands

Acquire your Land and become a LandLord, they will play a fundamental role in the game’s economy. A Land is a space inside ToysLand where you can build your empire as a merchant. To have the establishments it is necessary to have a Land.

Establishments

They are businesses that you can place in the Lands. These are an additional item, which means that you must buy a land and an establishment of your choice; be it an ammunition factory, battery factory or a market. Being the owner of an establishment, you should know that you will generate profits by being the intermediary, depending on your ability as a businessperson.

Key Features

Why are we different?

Circular Economy

Our ecosystem proposes a circular economy, where the players create added value during their game to make a profit. Players will obtain materials that must be processed in order to exchange them for TLC, in each processing a percentage of token is burned in game.

Original Design

Toys Legend has been designed with the intention of creating a fun and visually appealing game, so having an original design is a fundamental feature of the project. Therefore every skin, weapon and any 3D model and/or graphic piece in general has been made from scratch.

Play for Fun

This game has been thought and developed by people who enjoy videogames, so the main focus from the beginning has been to create a fun experience, where users can be entertained while generating income. Creating a balanced space for investors and video game lovers.
